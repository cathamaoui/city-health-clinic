# Healthcare Atlantic 🏥

Welcome to the official repository for the **Healthcare Atlantic** clinic. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to our community.

## 🕒 Hours of Operation
* **Monday to Saturday:** 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM
* **Sunday:** Closed

---

## 🩺 List of Services

### 1. Primary Care & General Medicine 
* **Routine Check-ups & Physicals:** Annual physicals, school, camp, and sports physicals.
* **Preventive Care:** Immunizations, vaccinations (including flu shots), and health screenings.
* **Chronic Disease Management:** Care for conditions like diabetes, asthma, and high blood pressure.
* **General Consultations:** Diagnosis and treatment for common illnesses.

### 2. Urgent & Acute Care (Minor Injury/Illness)
* **Minor Injury Care:** Treatment for sprains, strains, minor burns, and stitches.
* **Minor Illness Care:** Walk-in treatment for infections, fever, and digestive issues.
* **Procedures:** Wart removal and splinting.

### 3. Diagnostic & Laboratory Services 
* **Laboratory Tests:** Blood work, urine tests, and strep throat testing.
* **Imaging:** X-rays and ultrasounds.
* **Other Diagnostics:** ECGs/EKGs and vision/hearing screenings.

### 4. Women’s & Men’s Health
* **Sexual Health:** STI testing, treatment, and contraceptive counseling.
* **Gynecology:** Pelvic exams and Pap smears.
* **Prenatal/Postpartum Care:** Routine check-ups during and after pregnancy.
* **Men’s Health:** Prostate screening and health management.

### 5. Specialized & Mental Health Services
* **Mental Health:** Counseling for anxiety, depression, and PTSD.
* **Addiction Services:** Counseling and support for substance use.
* **Allied Health Services:** Nutrition counseling and physiotherapy.

### 6. Additional Support Services
* **Pharmacy:** On-site medication consultations.
* **Virtual Care:** Phone or video consultations for non-urgent issues.
* **Travel Health:** Vaccinations and consultations.
